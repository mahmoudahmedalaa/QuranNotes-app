Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to QuranNotes ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Information: If you sign in (via Apple, Google, or email), we store your email address and authentication identifier to sync your data across devices.
  • User-Created Content: Audio recordings, text notes, and folders you create are stored securely in the cloud (Firebase Firestore) to allow cross-device access.
  • Usage Data: We track basic app usage such as features used and consistency streaks to improve your experience. This data is not shared with third parties.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type and operating system version for crash reporting and performance optimization.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the QuranNotes service
  • Sync your content across devices
  • Send daily reminder notifications (only if you opt in)
  • Process subscription purchases via Apple's App Store
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, database storage, and crash analytics.
  • RevenueCat: For processing in-app subscriptions and purchases.
  • Expo: For delivering push notifications (only if you opt in).

5. No Ad Tracking

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third parties. We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA) or participate in ad networks.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL), secure authentication via Firebase Auth, and access controls to protect your data.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: All your notes and recordings are visible within the app.
  • Delete your account: Use the "Delete Account" option in Settings to permanently delete your account and all associated data (notes, recordings, folders, and settings).
  • Export your data: Contact us to request a copy of your data.

8. Children's Privacy

QuranNotes does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
